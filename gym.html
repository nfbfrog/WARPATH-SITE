For Gyms & Studios

Where gyms leak members, time, and follow-up.

Gyms have their own version of the same revenue problem: front-desk chaos, leads that never get chased, frozen members nobody reactivates, retail that drifts, and owners hunting for numbers. We audit that path, then build the fix.

What we inspect

  • Lead to member path — whether trials, consults, and new inquiries have a real owner and follow-up sequence.
  • Front-desk workflow — check-in, member lookup, waivers, issues, and how much staff has to remember.
  • Retention signals — past-due, frozen, inactive, and at-risk members surfaced before they quietly churn.
  • Classes, POS, and retail — the operational spots where money or time leaks daily.
  • Owner visibility — whether the gym has one clean view of members, revenue, tasks, and follow-up.
  • Warpath Gym fit — whether our existing gym platform can close the gaps faster than a custom build.

Why it's different

We are not only selling a gym app. We use the audit to decide whether you need Warpath Gym, a smaller follow-up build, a site fix, or just a few operational changes. The evidence chooses the scope.
