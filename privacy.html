How we handle information.

Warpath Collective is operated by NFB FROG Corporation, a Texas corporation. This policy explains what we collect when you contact us or use our services.

  • Information we collect: name, email, phone number, business information, website links, files you upload, project notes, and messages you send us.
  • How we use it: to respond to inquiries, deliver audits or software services, provide support, send requested follow-up, and improve our operations.
  • SMS consent: if you opt into text messages, we use your phone number only for Warpath-related inquiry, service, support, or scheduling communication. SMS opt-in is not sold or shared for third-party marketing.
  • Service providers: we may use providers for hosting, email, analytics, forms, CRM, payments, and messaging. They process information only as needed to provide those services.
  • Retention: we keep information as long as needed for business, legal, security, and service records.
  • Choices: you can request deletion or correction by emailing ethan@warpathcollective.com. For SMS, reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.
