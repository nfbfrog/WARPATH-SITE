For Small Business

Find where the jobs are slipping away.

For owner-led service businesses, the problem is rarely effort. It is missed calls, loose quotes, buried leads, no review process, and no clean view of the pipeline. We audit the revenue path, then build the system that keeps it from leaking.

What we inspect

  • Lead capture — whether the site, phone, form, and contact path make it easy to become a real lead.
  • Response time — what happens after a call, form fill, text, or message comes in.
  • Quote follow-up — whether estimates are chased or quietly left to die.
  • Pipeline visibility — whether the owner can see open leads, quotes, jobs, and revenue without asking around.
  • Reviews and reactivation — whether happy customers and past customers are being worked intentionally.
  • The build path — the CRM, automation, dashboard, site, or workflow needed to close the actual leaks.

Why it's different

We do not guess from a discovery call and sell you a bundle. We mystery-shop the path, score the leaks, show the screenshots, and rank the fixes. Evidence first. Build second.

How it works

01

Audit

We inspect the visible path, mystery-shop the business, and deliver a recorded walkthrough plus a written action plan.

02

Build

If you want us to fix it, we build the CRM, follow-up, dashboard, site, or automation the audit called for.

03

Retain

We keep the system clean with reporting, campaigns, maintenance, and monthly improvements.
