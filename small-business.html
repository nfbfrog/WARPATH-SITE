For Small Business

Stop running jobs from memory.

For service businesses, the problem is rarely effort. It is missed calls, loose quotes, buried leads, no review process, and no clean owner view.

What we inspect

  • Lead capture — site, phone, form, and contact path.
  • Response time — what happens after a call, form fill, text, or message.
  • Quote follow-up — whether estimates are chased or quietly left to die.
  • Pipeline visibility — open leads, quotes, jobs, revenue, and follow-up.
  • Reviews and reactivation — happy customers and past customers worked intentionally.
  • The build path — CRM, automation, dashboard, site, or tool needed first.
