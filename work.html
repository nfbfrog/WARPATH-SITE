Forged: a coaching platform and its client funnel

The coaching platform

Coach OS

A coach's whole roster in one branded app

Clients get a magic link — no password, no install. On their check-in day they submit required progress photos, weight, adherence, wellness flags, and the coach's own custom questions. Check-ins land in a review queue sorted worst-first, the coach answers with recorded video, and the queue auto-advances to the next one. Protocols, training splits, meal plans, messaging, and bloodwork trends all hang off the same client record. Built to run a full roster, not a spreadsheet.

Video check-ins

Feedback is recorded in the browser — camera, or a screen-share walking the client through their own charts. Every video is auto-transcribed, so the client's history stays searchable and nothing said in a review gets lost.

AI co-pilot

Every check-in arrives pre-briefed: weight stalls checked against adherence, wrong-direction trends, slipping compliance, wellness dips worth a lab panel, a body-fat estimate from the photos, and suggested talking points for the video. A full roster triages in minutes.

Training & meal builders

Weekly splits plus macro-controlled meal plans on a verified-food database — TDEE from real components, training-day and rest-day variants, iso-macro swaps, and grocery lists in quantities you can actually buy. Macros compute server-side, so plans are accurate by construction.

No migration, ever

The coach shares one join link and the existing roster registers itself. Clients drop their current plan docs — Word, Sheets, PDF, or pasted text — and AI splits them into protocol, training, and meal plan, ready for review. Moving a full roster takes an afternoon.

The client side

An installable app, not an app-store download. Protocol, training week, meal plan, weight trend, message thread, and every video the coach has sent — all in the coach's brand.

Progress reports

Branded, print-ready exports: before-and-after photos, the weight curve, and adherence averages — the coach's results page, generated from real data.
