Our own software

HomeGen

Listing video, automated. A real-estate agent uploads walkthrough footage from a showing and gets back a branded, captioned, post-ready video — vertical and widescreen — in minutes instead of a night in an editor. Synthetic demo data shown — no client accounts.

Footage in, reel out

A whole edit bay in a phone app

The agent shoots a walkthrough, adds the listing details, and taps once. HomeGen cuts the footage, writes captions, drops in title and outro cards in the agent's brand, and renders in every aspect ratio the channels need. What used to be a freelance-editor invoice and a two-day wait becomes a few minutes on the drive home.

Auto-edit pipeline

An async render engine handles the cutting, captioning, and encoding on its own infrastructure — the agent never touches editing software, and the phone is free the moment the upload finishes.

Branded, every time

Title and outro cards, colors, headshot, and contact details pull from the agent's brand kit, so every listing looks like their listing — not a template.

Reel & showcase modes

Fast social reels for reach and longer showcase cuts for the listing page, generated from the same footage in one pass.

Reusable listings

Saved listings re-render with new footage in a tap — a price drop or an open-house push doesn't mean starting over.
