Process

Know what you are buying before anything gets built.

Warpath starts with diagnosis. The Revenue Leak Audit gives you evidence, a written action plan, and a review call. Implementation only happens if the evidence calls for it and you choose it.

The audit path

01

Intake

We learn how leads, calls, forms, quotes, appointments, reviews, and repeat customers move through your business today.

02

Evidence

We trace the path from outside and inside where access is provided. Every finding is observed, screenshotted, measured, or labeled as an estimate.

03

Plan

You receive a Loom walkthrough, written action plan, screenshot evidence folder, ranked leak list, and DIY fixes.

04

Decision

You can execute the plan yourself, ask us to implement one piece, scope a larger system, or pause. The audit stands on its own.

Deliverables

  • Loom walkthrough - a recorded explanation of what we found and why it matters.
  • Written action plan - 2-4 pages ranking the leaks and the first changes to make.
  • Screenshot evidence folder - proof for every claim that can be observed from the audit path.
  • DIY fixes - useful changes you can make without hiring Warpath to implement.
  • Review call - 30 minutes to walk through the evidence and decide what should happen next.

Pricing bands

Founder Audit

Standard Audit

$1,250+ after the founder audits. Same standalone diagnosis, scoped to the business and access needed.

Focused Implementation

$2,500-$5,000 when one contained leak is worth fixing: missed calls, quote follow-up, forms, reviews, or dashboard repair.

System Build

$7,500-$20,000+ when the audit proves multiple connected leaks need one operating system.

Rules

  • The audit stands alone - you own the plan whether or not Warpath implements anything.
  • No pressure scope - implementation is only discussed after the evidence is reviewed.
  • No fake math - estimates are labeled as estimates.
  • No guaranteed ROI - we diagnose leaks and build systems, not promises we cannot prove.
  • No vendor blame - we focus on the path and the evidence, not drama.
