WARPATH Suite & systems

One business system, built in pieces.

CRM, dashboards, bots, portals, and internal tools for businesses outgrowing spreadsheets, inboxes, and disconnected apps. Start with the piece that hurts, then expand the setup when it is worth it.

How it is packaged

Core

WARPATH Suite

The full setup: business dashboard, CRM, bots, reports, site connections, and monthly cleanup.

Entry

CRM & Bots

The first systems most businesses need: missed-call recovery, quote follow-up, lead routing, reviews, and reactivation.

Custom

Business dashboard

A focused owner view for the numbers and work that keep getting chased by hand.

What gets built

  • CRM & pipeline - leads, quotes, jobs, owners, stages, and next steps.
  • Lead capture - forms, calls, DMs, and landing pages wired into one path.
  • Missed-call recovery - voicemail capture, AI summary, text-back, lead record, owner alert, and next touch.
  • Quote follow-up - open value, due dates, stale quote alerts, and touch history.
  • Review & reactivation - happy customers asked on time, past customers brought back.
  • Owner dashboard - leads, revenue, pipeline, follow-up health, and alerts in one view.

Built per business

Behind the scenes, WARPATH is built to run multiple businesses cleanly. Each client gets its own setup, data, features, and systems, so onboarding stays cleaner and expansion does not mean rebuilding from scratch.

Build the useful piece first.

Start with CRM & Bots, a dashboard, or the full Suite depending on where the business is losing time or money.
